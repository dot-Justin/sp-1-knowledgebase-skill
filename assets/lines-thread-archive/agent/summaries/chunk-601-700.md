## Narrative

**MIDI clock decode confirmed:** Galapagoose identifies the timing metadata definitively. The 16-bit counter values encode **MIDI clock position** using 4×24 PPQN (Pulses Per Quarter Note) - standard MIDI resolution. The MIDI 0xF8 timing clock message pulses at **31,250 baud** with **128μs intervals**, which maps exactly to the counter step distribution.

This means the timing field in every audio chunk encodes the musical beat position of that audio chunk - allowing the device to stay synchronized with external MIDI clock sources. The TE-SP-1 is not just a stem player; it's a MIDI-sync-aware device at the hardware level.

**Historical timeline published:** Community member **maybe** writes a comprehensive history of the TE-SP-1 - from original Kano/TE collaboration through Donda/Jesus Is King distribution through this reverse-engineering thread. This becomes the canonical historical reference (post 743).
