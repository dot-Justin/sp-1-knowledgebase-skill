## Narrative

**Custom stems on original TE firmware:** TimK confirms a result more significant than custom firmware - properly formatted audio written to the eMMC will play correctly on the **unmodified original TE firmware**. No custom firmware needed. No flashing required. Just write the correctly formatted binary and the device plays it.

This is the endgame: the format is fully documented, encoding tools exist, and anyone with a TE-SP-1 and the Track1+4 bootloader technique (or eMMC direct access) can load their own stems. The device has been fully unlocked.

**Final audio format summary published** by TimK and JoseJX - the definitive reference combining everything learned:
- Binary format, no header
- 8K sectors, 2K chunks
- 24-bit PCM, 48kHz, 8 channels interleaved
- Timing counter: 16-bit, range 0–24,489, 96 steps/cycle = 4×24 PPQN MIDI
- Works with original TE firmware unmodified

**Thread closure:** Lines forum moderators close the thread. The staff note added to post 1 cites "continual issues with problematic activity contrary to Forum Rules and Terms of Service" and interference with project maintainers' coordination. The community is directed to:
- **Discord:** discord.gg/y4V6VfHYck

The thread closure is read by many as IP-related pressure, given the involvement of copyrighted content and the prominence of the artists. The community moves without significant disruption - Discord had already become the primary real-time venue.
