Information about the different peripherals and how to access them.

Most examples I provide here are for the old (and unsupported) [nRF5 SDK v17.0.2](https://www.nordicsemi.com/Products/Development-software/nRF5-SDK/), because that's what I've been using. I believe these examples will also help you get started if you are choosing to use the current nRF Connect SDK.

## [I2S](i2s)
Setting up I2S to send audio to the 2 amplifiers and the Bluetooth module.

## [I2C / TWI](i2c)
I2C setup for controlling the TAS2505 and CS42l42 audio amplifiers.

## [PWM](pwm)
PWM setup for driving the LEDs.

## [SAADC](saadc)
SAADC setup for reading ladders, faders and battery level.

## [Battery charger](Battery-charger)
Interfacing with the BQ24232 battery charger.

## [Bluetooth module](Bluetooth-Module)
Interfacing with the bluetooth module.
