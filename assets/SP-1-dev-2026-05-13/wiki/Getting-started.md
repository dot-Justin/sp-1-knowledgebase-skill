
Getting started with developing for SP-1.

Thanks to the hard work of the engineers at Solderless, 
we can now load new firmware on the SP-1 without opening the device and accessing the SWD port.
It is risky to load new firmware onto a device without debugger access. Properly test new firmware and make sure it is compatible with SP-1 and its bootloader. 

Please see the instructions and warnings on the [Bootloader](./Bootloader) page.
